About

I have been devoting the majority of my free time to do what I love most: reading.
It is just wonderful to let myself lose into a whole new world every time I open a book. To me, reading is simply a great way to unwind at the end of a stressful day and enjoy my own imaginations.
Reading comes as the most useful facility for me to see this wonderful world through others' eyes, thus offering me the scenes of all over the world.
